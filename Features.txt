- Dynamic greeting (Good Morning/Afternoon/Evening based on time)
- Live time/date display
- Animated floating boxes (toy animation)
- KPI cards with trend indicators
- Quick Actions grid with hover effects
- Shows immediately after login

## Navigation Pages
1. Dashboard - Full analytics dashboard with charts
2. Riders - Manage rider information, status, photos, print/export
3. Attendance - Track rider attendance
4. Shipments - Manage shipment and rider assignments
5. Orders - View, edit, delete orders with filters
6. Customers - Customer management with contact cards
7. Inventory - Stock management with rack visualization mode
8. Returns - Return tracking with reasons
9. Revenue - Financial tracking by payment type
10. Reports - Analytics with bar graphs and charts
11. Settings - Theme picker, backup/restore
12. Contact - Contact form and developer info
